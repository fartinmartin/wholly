Our Mission

Using Cannabis to Overcome Climate Challenges

Modern agricultural practices have interrupted the earth's natural ability to recycle carbon, resulting in high concentrations of carbon making its way into our atmosphere and wreaking havoc on our global climate.

Cannabis is exceptionally good at pulling carbon out of the air and redirecting it back into soil, where it can be used by plants as healthy nutrition. By alternating their usual crops with cannabis each growing season, farmers can maintain healthier soil that grows more nutrient rich food and reduces the need for harmful pesticides.

Facing an Uncomfortable Truth

We believe that any modern cannabis company has a responsibility to acknowledge the vast disconnect between the amount of wealth being created in today’s booming legal marijuana industry and the destructive economic effects the war on cannabis has had –and continues to have– on over-policed communities.

This is why Wholly has partnered with the Prison Policy Initiative to build re-entry infrastructure into our hiring practices, focusing on hiring previously incarcerated individuals with non-violent drug related offenses on their records. This effort is a small but significant step in working toward a more equitable future for the marijuana industry.
